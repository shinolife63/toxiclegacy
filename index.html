What this is about

The Ford Motor Company operated a car manufacturing plant in the town of Mahwah, from 1955 to 1980, approximately 25 years. When it opened, in 1955, it was the largest car factory in the USA. From 1967 to 1974, the Mahwah Ford Motor plant, used the abandoned mine shafts of the nearby Ramapo Mountains, as a dumpsite for their toxic waste consisting of paint sludge, and solvents. The volume of toxic waste the plant produced and dumped during that time period was millions of gallons of paint sludge. ...Millions of gallons of paint sludge that was NEVER properly cleaned up.

35 Years later, in 2015, Ford Motor has still done very little to right their wrongs, and change is long overdue.
